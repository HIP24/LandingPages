Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

At Salah Stars, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. **Salah Stars is a local-only application: we do not use servers, we do not require accounts, and your data never leaves your device.** This Privacy Policy explains what data your device processes locally when you use our app.

No Data Collection or Transmission

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to external servers. All data related to your prayer habits and app usage is stored exclusively on your device's local storage.

Information Processed Locally

Prayer Data

The app processes and stores your prayer habits locally to help you track your spiritual discipline. This includes:

  • Prayer completion status (on-time, delayed, missed)
  • Prayer timestamps and dates
  • Stars earned or lost through our accountability system

Location Data

To calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction locally on your device, the app may access your device's location information (GPS, IP address, or Wi-Fi data). This access only occurs if you grant permission through your device settings. Your location data is used only for local calculations and is never sent to any server.

Device and Usage Information

Technical information (such as device model or app version) may be used locally for app optimization. Any crash reports or diagnostic data are only sent if you explicitly choose to share them via standard operating system prompts (Apple/Google), and we do not associate this with your identity.

How Your Information is Used

The data processed on your device is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the prayer tracking service locally
  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Generate your personal statistics and insights
  • Send local prayer time notifications and reminders

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in your device's secure local storage. Since we do not have a server or accounts, we do not have access to your data. Your data's security depends on your device's security settings (e.g., passcode, biometrics).

Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or rent any of your data.** Because your data is stored only on your device, we have no data to share. Your spiritual journey remains entirely private to you.

Your Rights

Since all data is local, you have full control over it. You can access and delete all your prayer data at any time by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the application. There are no accounts to delete as we do not store any identifying information.

Children's Privacy

Salah Stars is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
