Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

At Salah Stars, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our prayer tracking application.

Information We Collect

Prayer Data

We collect information about your prayer habits to help you track and improve your spiritual discipline. This includes:

  • Prayer completion status (on-time, delayed, missed)
  • Prayer timestamps and dates
  • Stars earned or lost through our accountability system
  • Prayer statistics and analytics

Device Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to ensure the app functions properly:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and performance data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the prayer tracking service
  • Generate personalized statistics and insights
  • Send prayer time notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your prayer data is stored securely on your device and, if you enable cloud sync, on encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal prayer data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your spiritual journey remains private. We may only share data in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your prayer data and statistics
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

Children's Privacy

Salah Stars is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
