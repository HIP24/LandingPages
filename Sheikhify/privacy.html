Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

In our pursuit of providing authentic spiritual guidance, Sheikhify treats your privacy as an Amanah (trust). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect the information you entrust to us.

Information We Collect

Chat and Voice Interactions

We collect information about your interactions with Sheikhify to provide accurate guidance. This includes:

  • Chat messages and conversation history
  • Voice interaction transcripts (transcribed from audio)
  • Note on Video: Sheikhify does NOT access, record, or share any camera or video data from your device. The 'Video' feature consists solely of an animated character providing responses.

AI Processing Data

To generate spiritual responses, your text and voice transcriptions are processed by trusted third‑party AI services (such as DeepSeek and ElevenLabs). We only share:

  • Your message text and derived transcripts
  • Recent conversation context (to maintain the flow of discussion)
  • General profile details you choose to provide (e.g., name)

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address and authentication credentials
  • Profile information
  • Subscription status

Device Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to ensure the app functions properly:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and performance data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide accurate spiritual guidance and answers
  • Maintain your personal conversation history
  • Generate responses using third‑party AI technology
  • Manage your account and subscription status
  • Respond to your support and feedback requests

Data Storage and Security

Your conversation data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only process data with trusted partners to provide the service:

  • AI Service Providers: We send your messages and transcripts to trusted partners, including DeepSeek for AI processing and ElevenLabs for voice generation and transcription, strictly to generate the AI's response.
  • RevenueCat & App Stores: Used securely to manage your subscription status.
  • Firebase (Google): Used to securely store your account and chat data.

We do not allow any third parties to use your data for advertising purposes.

AI Consent

Before any data is processed by AI services, you must provide consent inside the app. You can review our commitment to your privacy at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your conversation history inside the app
  • Delete your account and associated data - Learn how to delete your account

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
