Delete Your Account

Last updated: February 10, 2026

To delete your Sheikhify account and associated data (including chat history, user profile, and subscription data), follow these steps:

  1. Open the Sheikhify app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select 'Delete Account Permanently'.
  4. Confirm permanent deletion of account.

Data is deleted immediately upon confirmation. If you encounter any issues or need assistance, please contact us at halilpamuk24@gmail.com.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to back up any important data before proceeding.
